There Are EIGHT Combinations For MA

”The full form of MA is a Master of Arts. Master of Arts is a postgraduate course that generally takes two years to complete. Graduate candidates can only take an MA course. MA is a professional course that gives students a comprehensive knowledge of the topic they have selected”.

M.A. in Theatre Arts

M.A (Theatre) or Master of Arts in Theatre is a Post-Graduate Acting, Dance, and Drama course. Theatre is a building where theatrical performances or motion-picture shows can be presented.

M.A. in English

MA in English is a two-year postgraduate program in the field of Arts. The course covers the learning of English Literature, Linguistics, Criticism Theory, History of English Literature and various other concepts that form the basis of English Literature.

M.A. in Economics

The MA in Economics is a terminal master's program that equips its graduates with the advanced knowledge and quantitative skills required for an economic analyst in the business world, the consulting industry, a central bank or government.

M.A. in Political Science

MA (Political Science) is a postgraduate Political Science course. It is a two years programme and includes a comprehensive and thorough study of Political Science as a discipline at an advanced level.

M.A. in Sociology

What is an MA in Sociology? The Master of Arts (MA) in Sociology program prepares students in the broad skills typically utilized by contemporary sociologists. The goal is to provide students with advanced training beyond the undergraduate level for success in a number of professions as applied social scientists.

M.A. in Public Administration

M.A. in Public Administration is a two-year post-graduate programme offered by Social Science department of various universities across the country. The course offers specializations in a variety of subjects such as management, political science, economics, civics, law, among others.13-Mar-2023

M.A. in Telugu

M.A. in Tegulu is a postgraduate degree in Telugu literature and language, linguistic and Philosophy. Telugu is a Dravidian language spoken predominantly in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh, where it is also an official language.

M.A. in History

What is MA History? MA or Master of Arts in History is a two-year postgraduate degree programme divided into four semesters. It is the study of the past and current interpretations of the past.
